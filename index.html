About Us

Me and my friends love cars and motorsports and thought we would make a blog that shared our passion with others. We love cars and their mechanics, formula 1, stock car racing, and many other things to do with cars. Please enjoy our blog and website, send us any questions on the contact us page, or feel free to donate (although it is not necessary). You can also send us suggestions and ideas on the contact us page. Thanks for all of the support, we appreciate any and all donations.
